Hi, I'm Anith

I am a UX Designer / User Researcher / Wordsmith

I am a UX designer/developer with a passion for human-machine interactions. I have worked on various projects as the UX lead and/or as an interaction designer during the course of my study. I love human-computer interaction and I believe in solving real-life problems through the application of an intuitive and a practical approach to interface design.

I have also conducted usability testing and have done extensive research work on the same — both as part of my coursework, as well as independently.
